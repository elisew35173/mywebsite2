About Me

My name is Elise Nicole Wojack. I live in Brookfield, IL with my mom and my younger brother, Joseph. I attend Riverside Brookfield High School in, unsurprisingly, Riverside IL. I decided to apply for the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program because I found I really enjoyed the Girls Who Code club at my school. I didn't really have any coding experience before this, but I really have been enjoying it, and I'm looking forward to taking computer science classes next year.
